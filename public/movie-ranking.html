Madison's Must-Watch Movies

This is a list of my top 3 favorite movies of all time!

The Proposal

This is a great movie to watch if you're in the mood for a Rom-Com. Ryan Reynolds and Sandra Bullock make the perfect couple as they try to scheme their way into a promotion and staying in the country.

The Parent Trap

As a triplet, this movie hold s a special place in my heart. Two twins who never knew eachother existed meet at summer camp and come up with a plan to switch places to they each could meet the other parent they've never seen before. It is a classic.

Coco

This is a great family movie with amazing animation and music. Beautiful story of family and passion. The end will bring tears to your eyes.
